我们的故事

Our Story

Emoji Arcade 诞生于一个简单的想法：为什么玩游戏一定要下载 App、注册账号、等待加载？

Emoji Arcade was born from a simple idea: Why should playing games require downloading apps, registering accounts, and waiting for loading?

2026年初，一群热爱游戏和 Emoji 文化的开发者聚在一起，决定打造一款真正"即点即玩"的在线游戏平台。我们坚持使用最基础的 Web 技术——HTML5 Canvas + JavaScript，不依赖任何游戏引擎，确保每一款游戏都能在浏览器中流畅运行。

In early 2026, a group of developers passionate about gaming and Emoji culture came together to create a truly "instant play" online gaming platform. We stick to basic web technologies — HTML5 Canvas + JavaScript — without relying on any game engines, ensuring every game runs smoothly in the browser.

🎯 我们的使命

🎯 Our Mission

让每个人都能随时随地享受游戏的快乐。无论是在通勤路上、工作间隙，还是深夜失眠时刻，打开浏览器就能找到属于你的那款游戏。

To let everyone enjoy the joy of gaming anytime, anywhere. Whether commuting, taking a break, or during sleepless nights, just open your browser and find your game.

Game Lineup

目前 Emoji Arcade 已上线 7 款精心打磨的休闲游戏：

Currently, Emoji Arcade features 7 carefully crafted casual games:

  • 🗿 表情大乱射 - 街机射击，消灭搞怪 Emoji 敌人
  • 🛻 废土求生 - 末日生存，改装皮卡对抗 20 波怪物
  • 🧩 记忆翻翻天 - 经典翻牌，支持双人对战
  • 🏎️ 油门焊死 - 极简竞速，单手操控极速狂飙
  • 🚗 漂移狂魔 - 三车道跑酷，考验反应极限
  • 🏎️ 道具互坑赛 - 道具赛车，欢乐互坑
  • 🚜 翻车模拟器 - 物理爬坡，魔性翻车
  • 🗿 Emoji Shooter - Arcade shooting, destroy quirky Emoji enemies
  • 🛻 Emoji Survive - Apocalypse survival, mod your truck against 20 waves
  • 🧩 Emoji Match - Classic memory game, supports 2-player battles
  • 🏎️ Emoji Rush - Minimalist racing, one-hand speed control
